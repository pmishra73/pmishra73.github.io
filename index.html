// about me

Turning ideas into
remarkable products

I'm Prasant Mishra — a full-stack web developer, product designer, and educator with a passion for building meaningful digital experiences. I bridge the gap between beautiful design and powerful engineering.

From crafting pixel-perfect interfaces to architecting scalable backend systems, I bring end-to-end ownership to every project. My latest work — Inflection — is an AI-powered emotion intelligence platform that analyzes meetings using voice prosody and real-time transcription.

Beyond building, I teach — guiding students in coding fundamentals and running corporate training programs that equip teams with cutting-edge skills. I also speak at events on web development, AI product design, and the future of technology.
